About Warcraft 3

Warcraft 3: The Frozen Throne is a legendary real-time strategy game developed by Blizzard Entertainment. It was released in July 2002 and has since become a classic in the gaming world.

In Warcraft 3, players can choose from four distinct races: Humans, Orcs, Undead, and Night Elves. Each race has its own unique units, heroes, and strategies, making for exciting and diverse gameplay.

The game's rich storyline, memorable characters, and competitive multiplayer scene have kept it alive and beloved by gamers for years. Explore our website to learn more about the races, heroes, and lore of Warcraft 3!
